You are now looking at the pulse page.

Pulse let's you visualize your Test Driven Development flow and see you red-green-refactor cycle in close to real time.

You can also export your pulse sessions in png format to keep them

Especially useful when doing katas, pulse gives you the tools to measure your cadence.

Pulse is compatible with Java and groovy.
